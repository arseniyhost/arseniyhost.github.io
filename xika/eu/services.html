SERVICES

The Institute offers:

  • development of IVS and UVS systems as part of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • inspection of facilities, design and development of automated process control systems at newly constructed and modernized facilities;
  • implementation of design work on the reconstruction, improvement and implementation of automated process control systems by power units in operation and newly commissioned has been archived;
  • replication of developed systems as a whole and their individual parts;
  • development of operational documentation for information, software and technical support of industrial control systems with power units;
  • personnel training for the operation of ICS and ICS as part of the automated process control system of power units;
  • methodological and advisory assistance in the creation of industrial control systems in the energy sector;
  • maintenance of systems in operation.

The software under development undergoes a mandatory verification procedure. Software tests are carried out: at the unique testing ground of the State Enterprise "HIKA", at the manufacturer of technical equipment, at the power unit during commissioning. Support for OPC technology. Own unique SCADA system. The operating environment of UNIX and Windows OS software.

DEVELOPMENTS AND ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Development of methods for creating integrated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs, where jointly solving technological, economic and diagnostic control problems.
  • Development of methods for creating integrated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs, where jointly solving technological, economic and diagnostic control problems:
    • control and registration (collection and processing of information entered through the communication complex with the facility (CSR), information exchange on the unit’s local network, information exchange with external systems, information display, signaling of violations, control of generator parameters, registration (archiving and documentation) of information;
    • maintenance of protections and interlocks (monitoring of protections and interlocks (KZB), centralized testing of protections (COZ);
    • applied tasks (calculation and analysis of technical and economic indicators (TEC) of equipment, calculation of unmeasured parameters;
    • monitoring of safety parameters (continuous (in real time) displaying to the operator enough information to determine the safety status of the power unit, information support for the operator’s activities to prevent and localize emergencies, control the release of radioactivity during normal operation, in the event of and after design basis accidents);
    • information and reference (issuing recommendations on the management of technological equipment in transient operation of a power unit, providing reference information on technological equipment and means of industrial control systems of a power unit);
    • functioning support (diagnostics of the state of hardware and software of the IVS, management of the functioning of the IVS, metrological calibration of the measuring channels);
    • automated settings (generation of database structures, input, structuring and editing of source data, generation of configuration data.
  • Ecological monitoring of the environment, information interaction in the power unit-power plant-power system.
  • Development and implementation of a multifunctional integrated multilevel system of internal reactor control SVRK-M for power units with VVER-1000 reactors with redundancy of the most important functions and open for further expansion, which allows:
    • monitor technological processes in the reactor installation (RU) and maintain the parameters of these processes within the framework established by the regulations for safe operation of nuclear power plants;
    • timely detect the output of switchgear parameters that define the boundaries of safe operation beyond the permitted boundaries and alert operational personnel;
    • provide information support for optimizing the passage of technological processes of switchgear in transient conditions;
    • monitoring the performance and metrological characteristics of the measuring channels of the SVRK-M during operation of the power unit;
    • archiving the work of the reactor core and the state of the coolant of the 1st circuit.
  • Top-level IVS software consists of:
    • a complex of programs for displaying information on the screens of block operators;
    • a set of violation signaling programs;
    • a set of archiving and registration programs;
    • video preparation editor;
    • dynamic block editor;
    • mapping algorithm editor.

All programs are implemented in C ++ using the Qt library and object-oriented design methods. As a database management system, Firebird is used. System software (open source software) is based on the Linux Fedora operating system.

The software under development undergoes a mandatory verification procedure. Software tests are carried out: at the unique testing ground of the State Enterprise "HIKA", at the manufacturer of technical equipment, at the power unit during commissioning.
