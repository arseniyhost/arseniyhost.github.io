About us

State Enterprise "Kharkiv Scientific Research Institute of Integrated Automation" (SE "HIKA") was founded in 1992 on the basis of the All-Union Central Research Institute for Integrated Automation (Central Research Institute of Automation, Moscow) - is the lead institute of the Ministry of Energy and Coal Industry of Ukraine for the development and implementation of managers and upper-level information and computing systems (ICS and IVS) as part of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs.

The Institute has highly qualified scientific and technical personnel with many years of experience in creating and implementing the developed systems. The organizational structure of SE "HIKA" is represented by 4 research and production departments:

  • department №1 of mathematical and information support for the tasks of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department №2 of the top-level software of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department №3 of the functioning of the subsystems of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • branch №4 of the automated process control system of TPPs and NPPs.

The HIKA computing technology park includes 185 modern computers, servers and network equipment. Scientific and technical work of the institute is carried out on a unique high-tech equipment (training ground) with a total area of 385 m2, on which the development, modeling, commissioning and testing of the developed systems is carried out before their implementation at turnkey facilities.

Polygon Institute is:

  • workstations for modeling technological processes at power units;
  • workplaces of operators (RMOT);
  • archive server;
  • gateways of external systems;
  • MSKU-4 technical equipment complex for communication with external equipment and systems of nuclear power plants and thermal power plants;
  • synchrometer of system of uniform time.

Currently, SE “HIKA” performs:

1. Stage-by-stage modernization of the upper and lower IVS levels at NPP units with VVER-1OOO reactors in Ukraine and in Russia:

  • modernization of the IVS of the power unit with the integration of ASRK, ASKRO and SPBB of power unit No. 3 of the OP of the South Ukrainian NPP (Ukraine);
  • modernization of the TITAN-2 ICT by replacing software and hardware systems at power unit No. 4 of the Balakovo NPP (Russia);
  • technical support of the software of the IVS of power units No. 1-4 of the OP “Rivne NPP” (Ukraine);
  • modernization of the control unit of the air-conditioning unit of the power unit No. 5 of OP Zaporizhzhya NPP on the basis of the MSKU-4 TS.

2. Reconstruction and development of the IVS and automated process control systems of TPP units:

  • development and implementation of the upper level of the control unit of the unit in the part of the control and A boiler during the modernization of the dust preparation system power unit No. 6 of the Zmiyevskaya TPP of PJSC Centrenergo;

3. Development and implementation of support functions for the power unit operator, diagnostics of power equipment and full-scale simulators NPP power units.

4. Participates in the creation of crisis centers at nuclear power plants and a comprehensive (consolidated) program to improve nuclear safety power plants when extending the life of nuclear power units.
