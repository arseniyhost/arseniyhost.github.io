ABOUT US

State Enterprise "Kharkiv Scientific Research Institute of Complex Automation" (SE "KhICA") was founded in 1992 on the basis of the All-Union Central Research Institute for Integrated Automation (Central Research Institute of Automation, Moscow) - is the lead institute of the Ministry of Energy and Coal Industry of Ukraine for the development and implementation of managers and upper-level information and computing systems (CCS and CIS) as part of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs.

The institute has highly skilled scientific and technological staff with long-term experience of the creation and implementation of the developed systems. The organizational structure of SE "KhICA" is represented by 4 research and production departments:

  • department No. 1 of mathematical and information support for the tasks of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department No. 2 of the top-level software of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department No. 3 of the functioning of the subsystems of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • branch No. 4 of the automated process control system of TPPs and NPPs.

SERVICES

The Institute offers:

  • development of CIS and UVS systems as part of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • inspection of facilities, design and development of automated process control systems at newly constructed and modernized facilities;
  • implementation of design work on the reconstruction, improvement and implementation of automated process control systems by power units in operation and newly commissioned has been archived;
  • replication of developed systems as a whole and their individual parts;
  • development of operational documentation for information, software and technical support of industrial control systems with power units;
  • personnel training for the operation of CCS and CIS as part of the automated process control system of power units;
  • methodological and advisory assistance in the creation of industrial control systems in the energy sector;
  • maintenance of systems in operation.

PARTNERS

The creation and implementation of the systems developed by KhICA is carried out jointly with domestic and foreign organizations:

  • with the developer of technical controls of CJSC SNPO Impuls, ZAO NPO Radiy;
  • with the developer of technical controls of CJSC SNPO Impuls, ZAO NPO Radiy;
  • technological organizations (CJSC LvivORGRES, EMV-ENERGO, LLC Baltic Information Systems (Lithuania), LLC Moscow Plant Fizpribor.
