ABOUT US

State Enterprise "Kharkiv Scientific Research Institute of Integrated Automation" (SE "HIKA") was founded in 1992 on the basis of the All-Union Central Research Institute for Integrated Automation (Central Research Institute of Automation, Moscow) - is the lead institute of the Ministry of Energy and Coal Industry of Ukraine for the development and implementation of managers and upper-level information and computing systems (ICS and IVS) as part of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs.

The Institute has highly qualified scientific and technical personnel with many years of experience in creating and implementing the developed systems. The organizational structure of SE "HIKA" is represented by 4 research and production departments:

  • department №1 of mathematical and information support for the tasks of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department №2 of the top-level software of automated process control systems for power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • department №3 of the functioning of the subsystems of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • branch №4 of the automated process control system of TPPs and NPPs.

SERVICES

The Institute offers:

  • development of IVS and UVS systems as part of the automated process control system of power units of TPPs and NPPs;
  • inspection of facilities, design and development of automated process control systems at newly constructed and modernized facilities;
  • implementation of design work on the reconstruction, improvement and implementation of automated process control systems by power units in operation and newly commissioned has been archived;
  • replication of developed systems as a whole and their individual parts;
  • development of operational documentation for information, software and technical support of industrial control systems with power units;
  • personnel training for the operation of ICS and ICS as part of the automated process control system of power units;
  • methodological and advisory assistance in the creation of industrial control systems in the energy sector;
  • maintenance of systems in operation.

The software under development undergoes a mandatory verification procedure. Software tests are carried out: at the unique testing ground of the State Enterprise "HIKA", at the manufacturer of technical equipment, at the power unit during commissioning. Support for OPC technology. Own unique SCADA system. The operating environment of UNIX and Windows OS software.

PARTNERS

  • technological organizations (CJSC LvivORGRES, LLC EMV-ENERGO, LLC Baltic Information Systems (Lithuania), LLC Moscow Plant Fizpribor.
